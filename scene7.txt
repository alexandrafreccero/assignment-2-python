There is a magnetic pull radiating from it that seem to draw your feet and legs towards it.
It is similar to the sensation one gets when immersed in a river, 
and your feet are caught in an underwater stream. 
It is a pleasant and tingling feeling.
As you get closer to the disk the air seems to get warmer as if the disk was a living body. 
The disk is large but not huge.
You see that there are geometrical patterns on its surface, 
and that  one of the shapes, a square, is emitting a red light.
